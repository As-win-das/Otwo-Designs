OTWO DESIGNS IS A LEADING PROFESSIONAL WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT AND MOBILE APP COMPANY IN CALICUT (KOZHIKODE), IN KERALA, INDIA.

We specialize in customized, high-quality, affordable Website Design, Mobile Application Development, Web Hosting, Digital Marketing, Graphical Design, Logo Design, and Brand Consulting services in Calicut, Kerala. We provide tailor-made digital and communication solutions and the best web development solutions to clients in Kerala and all over the world.
OTWO DESIGNS was established in 2002 as a creative unit of OTWO Group Pvt Ltd, a leading IT solution company in Kerala, India with a dedicated team of the best web developers and web designers in Kozhikode. Over the years, the company has tremendously grown into the best website company in Calicut. Our highly-customized and targeted website-related services, branding services, and technical solutions have enabled businesses across the globe to establish their digital presence and develop strong brand identities. We thrive on constant innovation, creativity, and excellent customer relationships. We cater to professionals, businesses, and organizations of all sizes and in all segments.
